Child Safety & Protection Standards

Milaan has a zero-tolerance policy for any content, behavior, or activity that exploits, harms, or endangers minors. We define a minor as any person under the age of 18.

  • •

    Immediate action: We take immediate action against any content or behavior that sexualizes, exploits, endangers, or abuses minors

  • •

    Grooming prevention: Communications that attempt to manipulate, exploit, or build inappropriate relationships with younger users are prohibited and reported to authorities

  • •

    Legal compliance: We comply with all applicable laws regarding child safety and report illegal content to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant authorities

  • •

    Age appropriate: All content must be appropriate for users 13 and above; users under 18 have additional protection measures

  • •

    Prohibited content: Absolutely no content that depicts, promotes, normalizes or glorifies child abuse or exploitation in any form

  • •

    Urgent reporting: We provide multiple, easy-to-access reporting tools for users to flag concerning content or behavior involving minors

How We Protect Young Users:

  • • Age verification during registration
  • • Proactive content moderation
  • • Age-appropriate content filtering
  • • Regular safety audits and improvements
  • • Educational resources for safe online interaction
  • • Partnerships with child safety organizations

Respect and Inclusivity

Respect and Inclusivity

  • •

    Treat all community members with respect, dignity, and kindness

  • •

    Zero tolerance for discrimination based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or any other characteristic

  • •

    Bullying, hate speech, and targeted harassment will result in immediate account review

  • •

    Use inclusive language and remain mindful of diverse perspectives

  • •

    Particular attention is given to protecting younger users from any form of harassment or bullying

Authentic Interactions

Authentic Interactions

  • •

    Provide accurate information in your profile

  • •

    Use a real photo that clearly shows your face

  • •

    One account per person; multiple accounts are prohibited

  • •

    Verify your student status when requested

  • •

    No impersonating other students, faculty, or institutions

  • •

    Adults must never misrepresent their age or identity to interact with younger users

Appropriate Content

Appropriate Content

  • •

    Keep content relevant to academic and student life

  • •

    No explicit, adult, or inappropriate material

  • •

    Avoid graphic violence, gore, or disturbing content

  • •

    No promotion of dangerous activities or substance abuse

  • •

    Keep language professional and appropriate for an academic setting

  • •

    Content must be suitable for users aged 13 and above

  • •

    No sexualized content depicting or suggesting minors, even in cartoon, anime, or fictional form

Intellectual Property

Intellectual Property

  • •

    Only share content you own or have permission to use

  • •

    Properly credit sources and references

  • •

    No unauthorized sharing of course materials or paid content

  • •

    Respect trademarks and copyrighted material

  • •

    Do not share or sell academic work, assignments, or exam materials

Privacy and Security

Privacy and Security

  • •

    Never share personal contact information publicly

  • •

    Protect others' privacy; no sharing of private conversations

  • •

    No recording or screenshots without explicit permission

  • •

    Keep location sharing settings appropriate for your safety

  • •

    No collecting or harvesting user data

  • •

    Report security concerns immediately

  • •

    Users under 18 have enhanced privacy protections by default

Reporting Concerns

How to Report Concerns

If you encounter any content or behavior that violates our guidelines, especially concerns related to child safety, please report it immediately using one of these methods:

In-App Reporting

  • • Tap the three dots (⋮) on any post or message
  • • Select "Report" from the menu
  • • Choose the violation category
  • • For urgent child safety concerns, select "Child Safety"

Reports are reviewed 24/7, with child safety reports prioritized

Direct Email

Contact our Safety Team directly:

nextingames111@gmail.com

For urgent child safety concerns, include "URGENT" in the subject line

Immediate Concerns

If you believe a child is in immediate danger, please:

  • 1. Contact local law enforcement immediately
  • 2. Report to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children: CyberTipline or call 1-800-843-5678
  • 3. Then notify our Safety Team at 1-800-555-1234 (24/7 emergency line)

What Happens After You Report

  1. Our Trust & Safety team reviews the report (urgent child safety reports are prioritized)
  2. Appropriate action is taken according to our policies and applicable laws
  3. Law enforcement is notified when required by law or when a child is at risk
  4. You may be contacted for additional information if needed
  5. While we cannot share details of investigations, we will inform you when your report has been processed

Our Commitment

Violation of these guidelines may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent ban from the Milaan platform. We reserve the right to update these guidelines as needed to maintain a safe and productive learning environment.

Child safety is our highest priority. Violations of our child safety guidelines will result in immediate action, including account termination and reporting to appropriate authorities.
